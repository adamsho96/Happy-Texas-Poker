Contact Support Frequently Asked Questions How do I play with my friends? Tap Create Room on the main screen. You'll get a 6-character room code. Share that code with your friends, and they tap Join Room on their iPhone and enter it. Everyone with the code joins the same game. Can I play alone against bots? Yes. After creating a room, tap Add Bot in the waiting room to fill the table. Bots play with real hand evaluation and adjusted strategies for heads-up and multi-way play. Can someone join after the game starts? Yes. Anyone with the room code can join mid-game. They'll sit out the current hand and be dealt in starting on the next one. What if I run out of chips? When you're busted, a re-buy prompt appears so you can drop fresh chips in and keep playing. You can also re-buy any time from the top menu. What if the host leaves the game? Host duties automatically transfer to the next remaining player. The game continues without interruption. Is this real-money gambling? No. Happy Texas Poker is play-money only. There are no chip purchases, no real-money wagering, and no prizes. The app is purely for entertainment and practice. The game seems stuck on someone's turn. Make sure all players have a stable internet connection. If a player is using the app while the screen is off or in another app, their turn may stall. The 20-second turn timer will auto-fold them if they don't act in time. I found a bug or have a feature request. Will my chip tracker history be lost? The tracker keeps full P&L history for every player who joined the room, including players who quit. As long as the room stays alive, the history is preserved.
